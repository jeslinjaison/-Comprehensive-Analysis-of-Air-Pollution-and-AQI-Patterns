# -Comprehensive-Analysis-of-Air-Pollution-and-AQI-Patterns



## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes air quality data to uncover AQI trends, pollutant influences, seasonal patterns, and city-wise variations through exploratory data analysis (EDA) and data visualization using Python.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze AQI trends across different cities.
* Identify the pollutants that have the greatest impact on AQI.
* Examine seasonal variations in air quality.
* Compare pollution levels across AQI categories.
* Study relationships between major pollutants and AQI.
* Generate actionable insights through data visualization.

---

## 🛠 Tools and Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Plotly
* Jupyter Notebook

---

The dataset contains air quality measurements collected from multiple cities and includes:

* PM10

---

## 📊 Key Findings

* PM2.5 showed the strongest correlation with AQI.
* PM10 was the second most influential pollutant.
* Winter recorded the highest AQI levels.
* Summer exhibited the lowest pollution levels.
* Moderate AQI category accounted for the majority of observations.
* PM2.5 and PM10 concentrations increased significantly in Poor and Very Poor AQI categories.
* Cities showed noticeable differences in pollution levels.
* Particulate matter emerged as the primary driver of AQI variation.

---

## 📈 Results

### Seasonal AQI Analysis

* Winter: Highest average AQI
* Summer: Lowest average AQI
* Spring and Autumn: Moderate pollution levels

### Pollutant Impact

Strongest Contributors:

1. PM2.5
2. PM10
3. NO₂
4. O₃
5. NOx

### AQI Category Analysis

* Good and Satisfactory categories had lower pollutant concentrations.
* Poor and Very Poor categories showed elevated PM2.5 and PM10 levels.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

The analysis revealed that particulate matter, especially PM2.5 and PM10, plays a significant role in determining air quality. Seasonal trends indicate that air quality deteriorates during winter and improves during summer. The findings emphasize the need for targeted pollution-control measures focused on reducing particulate emissions to improve overall air quality.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus on reducing PM2.5 and PM10 emissions.
* Strengthen air quality monitoring during winter.
* Promote public transportation and cleaner fuels.
* Increase urban green spaces and tree plantation initiatives.
* Implement city-specific pollution control strategies.
* Use predictive models for AQI forecasting.
